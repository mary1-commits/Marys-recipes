Somali Rice & Meat Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g lamb or beef, cut into chunks
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp cardamom
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 4 cups water or broth
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© onions until golden brown.
  2. Add garlic, tomatoes, and spices, cooking for a few minutes.
  3. Add meat and cook until browned.
  4. Pour in water or broth and simmer until meat is tender.
  5. Add rice, stir well, and cover until cooked.
  6. Fluff rice and garnish with fresh coriander before serving.
